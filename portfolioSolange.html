About Me

My name is Solange, a junior at Southern Utah University (SUU) majoring in Computer Science with a focus on Information Architecture(IA). I am passionate about organizing complex information to improve usability and user experience. My studies and projects involve creating intuitive navigation systems, effective categorization, and ensuring content accessibility, which are critical in helping users find and interact with information efficiently.

Through my coursework and hands-on projects, I have developed strong skills in designing and implementing user-centric information structures that facilitate effective communication and interaction across digital platforms.
